About Us



CYC is a student group acting independently of the University of California. We take full responsibility for our organization and this web site.

CYC focuses on working with local business and nonprofits to foster support and success within our community. Through pro-bono projects students work hands on in close groups to craft effective strategies. Each team is supported by CYC's learning intensive workshops, MBA mentorship, and an expansive professional network.
